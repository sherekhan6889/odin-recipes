Witches' Brew

Description

The classic witches' brew from Macbeth. Perfect for fits of paranoia and distrust of everyone around you. It's so good, the forest will come calling.

Ingredients

  • Fillet of a fenny snake
  • Eye of newt
  • Toe of frog
  • Wool of bat
  • Tongue of dog
  • Adder's fork
  • Blind-worm's sting
  • Lizard's leg
  • Owlet's wing

Steps

  1. Boil and bake the snake fillet in a very large cauldron.
  2. Add to bone broth and boil some more.
  3. Keep right on boiling until the broth is bubbling all the way to hell.
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste for a charm of powerful trouble.
